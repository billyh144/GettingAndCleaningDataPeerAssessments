# Human Activity Recognition Using Smartphones Dataset Version 1.0

"tidy_data.txt" contains 180 rows and 68 columns. 

**subjects:** the subject who performed the activity. Its range is from 1 to 30.    

**activity_labels:** total six activity labels. They are "WALKING", "WALKING_UPSTAIRS", "WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS", "SITTING", "STANDING", and "LAYING".
